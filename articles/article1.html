I was on the NEWS! Some comments on Daily Mail are crazy

15/04/2022

A sugar glider sneaked into my flat earlier this year, and I contacted RSPCA to rescue this little creature since I wasn't able to find its owner. RSPCA did an interview with me and send the article to various media outlets, including the likes of ITVand Daily Mail.

This is the first time I've ever been on the news since I moved to London and I was surprised by the outreach of this story. Even a journalist from CBS in Canada contacted me showing interest of a possible radio interview.

There were some pretty interesting or even offensive (I don't take it seriously) comments under the Daily Mail article and I've picked out a few worst rated ones from the website.

- Dick Dudley: Wonder what they taste like? (40 downvotes)

- Tim Eforerevolt: He should just let it go, every other species seems to thrive in London. (43 downvotes)

- Ruthless London: Surprised he didn't eat the possum! (29 downvotes)

- FYall: Keep this story. If it bites him you may add it to the theories about the origin of Covid (12 downvotes)

Also a few best rated ones:

- MysteryMike: He should just let it go, every other species seems to thrive in London. (143 upvotes)

- Grumpy_Oldman: I can see why they should not be kept as pets, but it is a charming little animal. (120 upvotes)

- George Roper: Poor creature, obviously got in an Uber when it was drunk. (52 upvotes)

- lots-of-fakes-onsite: Good job Mr Zoe did not have a cat or it would soon have been a dead Possum. (47 upvotes)
